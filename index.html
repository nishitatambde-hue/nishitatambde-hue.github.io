Before & after

Flat layouts vs water-glass surfaces.

Same content, different feeling. One looks like another template. The other feels like a product.

Flat layout
Looks “website-standard”.
  • Hard edges, low depth, everything sits on the same visual layer.
  • Cards compete with background gradients and imagery.
  • Users can’t tell what is interactive vs decorative.
GlassFlow layout
Feels like part of an OS.
  • Matte black base with floating glass panels and soft, layered depth.
  • Droplets, glow and tilt hint at a physical object under the cursor.
  • Interactive elements sit on dedicated, frosted surfaces.
